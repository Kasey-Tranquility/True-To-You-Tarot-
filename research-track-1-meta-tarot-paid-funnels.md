## Excluded / Did Not Qualify

- Seeer: Meta ads mention Tarot readings, but the destination is a broad psychic chat marketplace, not a Tarot-specific quiz/funnel.
- Spiritual Glows / Official Psychic Marie: Meta ads lead to `officialpsychicmarie.com/win/quiz`, but the visible ad and destination could not be verified as Tarot-specific pre-opt-in; appears broader psychic/love reading.
- Generic psychic brands such as Keen, Kasamba, Purple Garden, and Psychic Source: excluded unless a Tarot-specific quiz/funnel destination was visible.

## Notes Worth Stealing

- Best clean quiz tripwire: Biddy Tarot. Quiz result page moves naturally into a $19 confidence kit, then broader course/community ladder.
- Best aggressive report funnel: Tarot Element Reading. The mechanics are strong but the language is risky.
- Best ethical-feeling free challenge: The Sisters Enchanted. Immediate lead magnet stack, live event, and membership bridge without hard prediction claims.
- Best low-ticket reading ladder: Spiritual Society. Pick-a-card to $19.99/$24 readings to $33.33/mo membership.
- Best app install pattern: Salvia/Healer. Free minutes or tiny credit offer gets the install, then monetization happens through credits, calls, and subscriptions.
