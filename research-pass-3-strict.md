# Research Pass 3 Strict: Astrology/Tarot Funnel Models

Date: 2026-06-02

Scope: high-performing astrology, tarot, and spiritual guidance businesses that are not broad psychic marketplaces. Included businesses have at least one sophisticated funnel pattern: paid traffic, quiz, app, subscription, email capture, paid report ladder, or course/community monetization. Psychic marketplace-first operators such as Keen, Kasamba, Psychic Source, and Purple Garden are intentionally excluded.

## 1. Hint

Source links: [Web2App World funnel breakdown](https://web2appworld.com/breakdowns/hint/), [Hint pricing](https://hint.app/pricing), [Hint official site](https://hint.app/)

Landing page quality: highly optimized direct-response, not especially trust-building. The core creative asset is a blurred soulmate sketch and promise of a reveal. It uses social proof, press-style credibility, live counters, fast quiz pacing, and a persistent curiosity gap. The page quality is high in conversion terms, but low in brand maturity.

Offer economics: Web2App World estimates $3.8M/month in paid spend and describes a $1 trial into $29.99/month subscription, with 13 post-purchase upsells from roughly $1 to $19.99. Hint's pricing page confirms a $1 trial and monthly plan starting at $29.99/month.

Conversion mechanics: 31-screen quiz, birth/personality/relationship inputs, email capture before paywall, blurred result preview, trial pricing, then micro-upsell ladder. Strongest mechanics are sunk cost, mystery product, and progressive personalization.

Brand style: glossy, romantic, emotionally urgent, mass-market. More “reveal your destiny” than “reflective guidance.”

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium. Borrow the quiz-to-personalized-reading architecture, preview/paywall sequencing, and email capture. Avoid soulmate-sketch claims, artificial urgency, and the psychic-artist framing.

## 2. Nebula

Source links: [Web2App World funnel breakdown](https://web2appworld.com/breakdowns/nebula/), [Nebula about page](https://www.asknebula.com/about-us), [Nebula iOS app estimate](https://trendapps.dev/app/ios/1459969523/)

Landing page quality: extremely optimized, but aggressive. The funnel uses many persona-style entry points and segmented themes like soulmate, marriage compatibility, aura, motherhood, ex-return, and spiritual identity. Good lesson: one product can support many narrow acquisition hooks.

Offer economics: Web2App World reports 18.6M web visits in January 2026, estimated $6.8M/month YouTube spend, a $1 trial into $42.99/month, $14.99-$29.99 reading upsells, $9.99-$99.99 chat credit packs, and a 70% off cancellation-save offer.

Conversion mechanics: 51-screen quiz, processing screens, identity labels, paid web checkout, app download, upsells, and an aggressive cancellation flow. Uses high-volume creative testing across many landing pages and personas.

Brand style: cosmic app plus direct-response personalization. Broad spiritual self-discovery wrapper, but performance funnel is dominated by romance/future-curiosity hooks.

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium-low. Useful for segmentation strategy and “many entry hooks, one core product.” Poor fit for values if True to You Tarot wants grounded, consent-forward, non-manipulative guidance.

## 3. CHANI

Source links: [CHANI pricing support page](https://chaninicholas.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500001732281-App-Pricing), [CHANI Android revenue estimate](https://trendapps.dev/app/android/com-chani_nicholas_inc-chani/), [CHANI official site](https://www.chani.com/)

Landing page quality: premium editorial/wellness, not hard-sell. CHANI feels closer to a spiritual media subscription than a psychic product: rituals, meditations, workshops, affirmations, chart readings, and guidance for week/year ahead.

Offer economics: CHANI lists $11.99/month or $107.99/year after free trial options. TrendApps estimates Android performance at 8K last-30-day downloads and $100K revenue, with $12.50 revenue per download and 4.9 rating. Rev.now separately ranks CHANI first in its 2026 astrology app revenue list.

Conversion mechanics: app-first subscription, free trial, premium content library, weekly recurring rituals, annual discount, trusted expert/founder authority. Conversion depends less on one-time curiosity and more on content quality plus ritual retention.

Brand style: polished, feminist, wellness/editorial, human-authored, emotionally intelligent. High trust.

Fit for True to You Tarot: High. Best model for ethical recurring tarot: weekly/monthly ritual readings, paid member library, audio/video guidance, reflective exercises, annual subscription, and founder-led authority.

## 4. Labyrinthos

Source links: [Labyrinthos App Store page](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/labyrinthos-tarot-reading/id1155180220), [Labyrinthos official site](https://labyrinthos.co/)

Landing page quality: strong tarot-native product experience. The App Store listing emphasizes tarot learning, daily wisdom, journaling, custom spreads, reminders, ad-free experience, and deck aesthetics. It feels more product-led than funnel-led.

Offer economics: App Store listing shows 22K ratings at 4.9, in-app purchases, Premium at $9.99 for unlimited personalized readings, and deck add-ons commonly at $4.99. The listing also notes a large free-user base and the cost pressure that led to journal limits.

Conversion mechanics: daily use loop, tarot education, reminders, journaling limits, premium AI/personalized readings, digital deck purchases. The funnel is less “take quiz, pay now” and more “build a habit, then upgrade.”

Brand style: illustrative, gentle, educational, reflective, self-guidance oriented. Not psychic-marketplace-coded.

Fit for True to You Tarot: High. Strong model for a daily/weekly tarot ritual, card journal, spread library, learning pathway, paid prompts, and deck/product attach.

## 5. Biddy Tarot

Source links: [Biddy Tarot card meanings page](https://biddytarot.com/tarot-card-meanings/), [Biddy Tarot membership support](https://support.biddytarot.com/hc/en-us/categories/360000150855-Membership), [Biddy Tarot community](https://community.biddytarot.com/)

Landing page quality: strong SEO authority and education funnel. The card meanings page has a clear beginner promise, deep content, and an embedded “Tarot Reader Archetype” quiz lead magnet. The ecosystem includes books, decks, courses, certification, community, business coaching, and free readings.

Offer economics: diversified ladder rather than a single subscription: free SEO content, quiz/email capture, books/decks, courses, membership/community tiers, certification, and business coaching. Membership support confirms free community plus paid Collective-style tiers and practice reading exchange.

Conversion mechanics: SEO intent capture, quiz segmentation, free resources/PDFs, course ladder, community retention, peer practice exchange, certification credibility. This is the most mature tarot-specific education and email funnel model found.

Brand style: bright, approachable, teacher-led, polished but not luxury. Clear “learn to trust your intuition” voice.

Fit for True to You Tarot: High. Best model if True to You Tarot wants to combine readings, education, email list growth, quiz segmentation, and membership without becoming an app company.

## 6. Moon Reading

Source links: [Moon Reading shop](https://www.moonreading.com/shop/), [The Moon Reading free report page](https://themoonreading.com/), [MoonReading community/email prompt](https://www.moonreading.com/shop/)

Landing page quality: classic direct-response spiritual report funnel. Product pages and shop are less sophisticated visually than Hint/Nebula/CHANI, but the offer ladder is clear: personalized reports, love readings, transit guides, soulmate drawing, chakra program, email delivery.

Offer economics: shop lists digital reports from $11 and $19, a $67 transit guide, $99 10-year guide, $49 chakra program, and $37 soulmate story/drawing. Order links route through ClickBank-style checkout, suggesting affiliate/direct-response economics.

Conversion mechanics: free personalized reading, email delivery, report unlocks, upsell ladder by life area, daily readings/horoscopes email signup. Strongest lesson is “one personalized intake can generate many paid report SKUs.”

Brand style: mystical, direct-response, older-web polish. More offer-heavy than brand-heavy.

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium. Useful for one-time tarot report economics: low-ticket entry reading, love/career/year-ahead upgrades, email delivery, and post-purchase offers. True to You should make the page more tasteful, transparent, and human.

## 7. Co-Star

Source links: [Co-Star App Store page](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/co-star-personalized-astrology/id1264782561), [Axios funding/downloads article](https://www.axios.com/2021/04/14/astrology-app-co-star-raises-15-million-funding), [Co-Star official site](https://www.costarastrology.com/)

Landing page quality: iconic minimalist app-first presentation, but the App Store page is the better conversion surface. Strong social proof: Editors' Choice, 205K ratings at 4.8, press/famous-user quotes, and memorable brand language.

Offer economics: Axios reported 20M+ downloads and $15M Series A in 2021. App Store lists in-app purchases including Pro-Star monthly at $8.99, Eros at $6.99, question packs, year-ahead reports, crush reports, and advanced chart products.

Conversion mechanics: daily push notifications, friend compatibility, social graph, IAP add-ons, relationship reports, question packs. Co-Star's genius is social retention, not a paid traffic funnel.

Brand style: stark, edgy, witty, Gen Z, sometimes abrasive. Very distinct.

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium. Borrow the daily micro-message habit, compatibility/social sharing, and a bold voice. Avoid harsh prompts and over-ironic emotional guidance.

## 8. The Pattern

Source links: [The Pattern official site](https://www.thepattern.com/), [The Pattern subscription support](https://thepattern.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/14045427454740-General-information-about-Subscriptions), [The Pattern terms](https://www.thepattern.com/terms-of-service)

Landing page quality: clean, calm, depth-oriented. The Pattern avoids heavy astrology jargon and sells “understand yourself” through natal charts, bonds, transits, romantic transits, time-travel, and in-depth features.

Offer economics: subscription support confirms auto-renewing Go Deeper+ quarterly/annual and Connect+ quarterly. The exact pricing was not visible in the support article, but the app monetizes through paid subscriptions.

Conversion mechanics: birth chart intake, relationship/bond segmentation, transits as recurring reasons to return, premium depth reports, app download CTA. Strong retention logic: users return when relationships or timing questions feel active.

Brand style: sophisticated, introspective, minimalist, psychology-adjacent. Less mystical than most astrology brands.

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium-high. Good reference for non-cheesy self-understanding language, relationship segmentation, and “patterns” framing for tarot spreads.

Brand style: accessible, mainstream, content-heavy, more generic than CHANI/Biddy/Labyrinthos.

Fit for True to You Tarot: Medium. Borrow the daily email rhythm and segmented offer cadence. Upgrade the brand voice and avoid clutter.

## 10. Sanctuary

Source links: [Sanctuary official app page](https://shop.sanctuaryworld.co/pages/our-app), [Sanctuary FAQ](https://www.sanctuaryworld.co/faq/), [Sanctuary main site](https://www.sanctuaryworld.co/)

Landing page quality: modern mystical, millennial-friendly, press-backed. Strong app positioning: daily horoscopes, birth chart readings, guides, live readings, tarot, crystals, compatibility, and yearly forecasts.

Offer economics: Sanctuary+ includes content library access, a 5-minute expert reading, expanded horoscopes, tarot/crystal/yearly forecast content, and unlimited compatibility calculator. FAQ confirms subscription is iOS-only currently and renews via Apple.

Conversion mechanics: free app content, paid subscription, bundled expert reading credit, expanded daily/weekly/yearly content, compatibility tool. It is borderline because live expert readings make it marketplace-adjacent.

Brand style: friendly, polished, contemporary, “astrology you text with.”

Fit for True to You Tarot: Low-medium. Use as reference for packaging an expert reading credit inside a subscription. Do not copy the on-demand reader marketplace model.

## Best Strategic Lessons For True to You Tarot

1. Build a quiz-first intake, but make it ethical.
   - Best references: Hint, Biddy, Moon Reading.
   - Recommended quiz: “What kind of clarity are you seeking right now?” with paths for love, career, self-trust, transition, and spiritual growth.
   - Capture email before the result preview, but make the promise transparent.

2. Use one intake to create a paid offer ladder.
   - Best references: Moon Reading, Nebula, Biddy.
   - Suggested ladder: free mini pull, $11-$19 written mini reading, $37-$67 deeper recorded/video reading, $99+ seasonal/year-ahead spread, membership for monthly guidance.

3. Create recurring value through ritual, not fear.
   - Best references: CHANI, Labyrinthos, Astrology Answers.
   - Suggested recurring offer: monthly “True to You Circle” with collective card reading, journal prompts, moon/tarot ritual, and one rotating mini-spread.

4. Make the brand feel human and premium.
   - Best references: CHANI, The Pattern, Labyrinthos.
   - Avoid the overly synthetic soulmate/reveal style unless positioned as entertainment.

5. Segment by emotional job-to-be-done.
   - Best references: Nebula, The Pattern, Hint.
   - Strong tarot segments: “I need closure,” “I need courage,” “I need direction,” “I need to trust myself,” “I need relationship clarity.”

6. Email is likely the best first growth engine.
   - Best references: Biddy, Astrology Answers, Moon Reading.
   - True to You can compete without app development by using SEO pages, a quiz, a daily/weekly email, evergreen offers, and seasonal launches.
